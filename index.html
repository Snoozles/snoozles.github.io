About Me

My name is Susanne Brauer and I'm an aspiring Fullstack developer. As a former mechanical engineer from the automotive sector I have worked on aquiring coding skills for a while in order to be prepared to move in to the webdevlopment sector as I am excited for new opportunities in a new area.

In my previous career within product developmenet in the automotive secto I was able to develop strong problem solving skills as well a strutured way of working translating a variety of customer requriements into the supporting attribute characteristics. Additionally I got very familiar working in cross functional teams for the best possible results for the product and customers.

I'm looking forward to transfering the skills from my engineering experience and using my newly gained developer skills on new exciting opportunities.
